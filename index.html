About Us

Mission Statement at Patient's Choice Home Health Care

  • To provide the most compassionate professional home health care in the least intrusive manner

  • To assist our patients with all of their home bound needs while giving nothing less than Loyalty, Trust, Compassion and Respect.

Our History

Patient's Choice Home Health Care was created based on the patient's and family's point of view of what quality health care should be. We tailor our services according to the specific needs of each client – this makes individualized care more effective, convenient and less costly.

Our company was founded in 2005 by a team of specialists with more than 30 years of health care experience combined.

Our Commitment

At Patient's Choice Home Health Care, we highly emphasize efficient communication between the patient, the clinicians and physicians involved. We continually self-evaluate the quality of care provided and will look for ways to improve. It is our greatest commitment to educate patients on proper self-management of disease processes and ensuring safety at home.

Our Team

Patient's Choice Home Health Care is committed to reinforcing its staff's clinical knowledge and keeping them informed on best practices. We provide the most current home health care training and education to all of our staff members. Proper procedures are always in place for the coordination of patient care to meet specific needs, conditions and settings for our patients.

To preserve the quality of our home health care programs, we employ the most qualified and compassionate professionals. We comply with all State and Federal Licensing Requirements. We make certain, that prior to initial patient care, our staff is cleared by background and license screenings. Patient safety and confidentiality is of utmost importance at Patient's Choice Home Health Care.

Non-Discrimination Policy

Patient's Choice Home Health Care promotes equality in providing services and employment. In observation of Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, the Age Discrimination Act of 1975 and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, the company ensures that all eligible clients shall receive services due without unlawful discrimination against age, gender, color, religion, national origin, disability, race, sexual orientation or ancestry.

Professional Standards

Our professionals at Patient's Choice Home Health Care are governed under the State and Federal Licenses to provide the highest quality of services to our patients and their families based on company policies and procedures.
