About Us

Locally Trusted. Family-Focused. Clinically Strong.

Patients Choice Home Health Agency was founded with a simple mission to provide high-quality, compassionate health care to individuals in the comfort of their homes.

Our Community

As a locally owned and operated agency, we understand the unique needs of our community. We serve patients across Miami-Dade and Monroe Counties, from urban centers to coastal communities.

Our Team

We are proud to be a Medicare-certified and AHCA-licensed home health agency. Our team includes:

  • Registered Nurses (RNs)
  • Licensed Practical Nurses (LPNs)
  • Physical & Occupational Therapists
  • Home Health Aides (HHAs & CNAs)
  • Medical Social Workers

Each member of our staff is carefully screened, trained, and supervised to ensure our patients receive only the most reliable and compassionate care.

Our Philosophy

We treat every patient as family. We listen carefully, communicate clearly, and personalize every service around the goals of the patient and their family.

Our Services

Patients Choice offers a wide range of Medicare-certified and insurance-covered home health services, plus private-pay packages for postoperative and cosmetic surgery recovery.

Diabetic Care

  • Blood sugar monitoring
  • Medication/insulin administration
  • Diabetic foot care
  • Dietary and lifestyle education
  • Prevention of complications

Postoperative Recovery

  • Pain and wound management
  • Mobility assistance
  • Medication administration
  • Communication with your surgeon
  • Hospital-to-home transition planning

Cosmetic Surgery Recovery

  • Transportation to/from surgery
  • 24-hour post-op care
  • Lymphatic drainage support
  • Dressing changes, drain monitoring
  • Private nursing & concierge care

Core Home Health Services

  • Skilled Nursing: Wound care, IV therapy, injections, disease management
  • Physical Therapy: Post-surgical rehab, mobility, strength, balance
  • Occupational Therapy: Activities of daily living, adaptive techniques
  • Home Health Aides: Bathing, grooming, meals, mobility, light housekeeping
  • Medical Social Work: Counseling, community resources, care planning

All services are delivered under the supervision of your physician and based on your personalized care plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and care.

What is home health care?

Medical care provided in your home by nurses, therapists, and aides under a physician's direction.

Who qualifies?

Patients who are homebound and require intermittent skilled care.

Is it covered by insurance?

Yes! Medicare, Medicaid, and most private insurances cover qualified services.

Do you offer private-pay services?

Yes – especially for cosmetic recovery and concierge care.

How do I start?

Call us or have your doctor send a referral. We'll handle the rest!
