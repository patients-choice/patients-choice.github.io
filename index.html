Welcome to Patients Choice Home Health

Where compassionate care and clinical excellence come together in the comfort of your home. For over 20 years, we've proudly served the residents of Miami-Dade and Monroe Counties, bringing skilled nursing, rehabilitation, and personal care directly to our patients' homes.

At Patients Choice, we believe that healing happens best at home – where patients feel secure, supported, and surrounded by family. Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or simply need help with daily care, we are here for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and care.

What is home health care?

Medical care provided in your home by nurses, therapists, and aides under a physician's direction.

Who qualifies?

Patients who are homebound and require intermittent skilled care.

Is it covered by insurance?

Yes! Medicare, Medicaid, and most private insurances cover qualified services.

Do you offer private-pay services?

Yes – especially for cosmetic recovery and concierge care.

How do I start?

Call us or have your doctor send a referral. We'll handle the rest!
